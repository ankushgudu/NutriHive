Banana Oat Pancakes

Veggie-Cheese Muffins

How to Make It

  • Preheat oven to 180°C.
  • Mix flour, eggs, veggies, and cheese.
  • Bake for 20 minutes.
  • Let cool and enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

What foods should I avoid for better health?

Limit processed foods, sugary drinks, trans fats, excessive salt, and alcohol to maintain good health.

Can I consult a nutritionist on NutriHive?

Yes! NutriHive connects you with qualified nutritionists for personalized consultations and dietary guidance.

Is NutriHive suitable for special diets (vegan, keto, diabetic)?

Absolutely! NutriHive offers customized meal plans for various dietary preferences and health conditions, including vegan, keto, gluten-free, diabetic-friendly diets, and more.

Are gluten-free diets necessary for everyone?

No, only people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity need to avoid gluten. Others can include whole grains like wheat and barley in their diet.
